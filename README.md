# Anonymous Mental Health App

A privacy-focused Flutter-based mobile application designed to support mental well-being through anonymous self-help tools, mood tracking, journaling, relaxation exercises, and accessibility features.

## Overview

Mental health challenges such as stress, anxiety, and emotional pressure are becoming increasingly common. Many individuals hesitate to seek support due to privacy concerns and fear of judgment.

The **Anonymous Mental Health App** provides a secure and anonymous platform where users can monitor their emotional well-being, practice self-care, and access supportive resources without revealing personal identity.
